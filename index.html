          ⏱ Short-Term Goals

          Drive your immediate progress and build momentum step-by-step.

          ⚡

          Manage Objectives

            Core Philosophy

            Timeframe: days → weeks → 1–3 months
            Purpose: immediate progress + momentum
            Type: specific, actionable, measurable
            Connection Node: Short-term goals are the concrete *steps* required to mathematically reach your ultimate long-term destination.

            Examples

            • Finish 20 LeetCode problems
            • Build 1 small Unreal project
            • Study 2 chapters before exam
            • Exercise 20 days in a row

            👉 Think: "What can I complete soon?"

            VISION ARCHITECTURE

            📆 Long-Term Goals

            Establish your life outcomes and set your overall directional compass.

            ✨

            Visionary Destinations

              Core Philosophy

              Timeframe: 6 months → multiple years
              Purpose: direction + total life outcome
              Type: massive, vision-based, broken into small steps
              Connection Node: Long-term goals identify the ultimate *destination*. E.g., Long-term → "Become game developer". Short-term → "Finish 3 projects".

              Examples

              • Become a professional game developer
              • Build and publish your own game
              • Start earning from freelancing
              • Build a strong portfolio globally

              👉 Think: "Where do I want to be?"
